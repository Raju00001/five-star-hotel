Our Story

A Century of Unmatched Elegance

Since 1924, Grand Aurum has stood as the pinnacle of hospitality — where Art Deco splendor meets contemporary luxury, and every guest is treated as royalty.

Nestled in the city's most prestigious address, our 248-room masterpiece features curated art collections, Michelin-starred dining, and a spa that redefines wellness. From the grand marble lobby to the sky-high rooftop pool, we craft moments that last a lifetime.
